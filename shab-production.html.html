SHAB PRODUCTION is a full-service Creative Digital Video Production company headquartered in New Delhi. For over 12 years, we've been turning raw ideas into compelling visual narratives — working with brands, broadcasters, creators, and agencies across India and beyond.

From a single-person interview to a multi-city feature film shoot, we bring the same level of passion, precision, and creative fire to every production. Our team thrives on collaboration, and when you work with us — you'll feel that enthusiasm from day one.

Our Production Process

A seamless journey from your first idea to the final frame.

01

Discovery & Brief

We deep dive into your vision, goals, target audience, and budget to craft a crystal-clear production brief.

02

Creative Development

Scripting, storyboarding, concept art and creative direction — every detail planned before a camera rolls.

03

Pre-Production

04

Production
